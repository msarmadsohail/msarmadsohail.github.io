# Research & Publications

## IEEE WCNC 2024  
### **Optimizing Resource Allocation in MEC-Enabled CR-NOMA-Assisted IoT Networks: A DRL-Driven Strategy**

Published at **IEEE Wireless Communications & Networking Conference (WCNC 2024)**, Dubai.

This work proposes a DDPG-based dynamic resource allocation mechanism for IoT devices performing computation offloading under a MEC + CR-NOMA framework.

**Authors:**  
Muhammad Taha Qaiser, *Muhammad Sarmad Sohail*, Minahil Shafqat, Syed Asad Ullah, Haejoon Jung, Syed Ali Hassan  

Full Code + Paper: https://github.com/msarmadsohail/ddpg-mec-noma-resource-allocation
